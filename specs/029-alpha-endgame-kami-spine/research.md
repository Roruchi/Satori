# Research: Alpha Endgame Kami Spine

## Decision: Void-separated calm water island plus Suijin is the alpha finale

**Rationale**: The user explicitly corrected the final loop. This supersedes expansion-only acceptance.

## Decision: Suijin is the first alpha kami

**Rationale**: The user selected Suijin by defining a water/no-fire/Satori 1000 Chi+Ku invitation condition.

## Decision: Ku Seed places Void and Void separates islands

**Rationale**: Ku's first practical world effect is spatial: it creates void that breaks island continuity and makes island-local rules understandable.

## Decision: Suijin invitation uses Chi+Ku on a calm water island

**Rationale**: The player must place the Chi+Ku biome on an island with at least 10 water tiles, no fire-based tiles, and local Satori 1000. This makes the Suijin invitation a culmination of water shaping, restraint, and harmony.

## Decision: Keep Suijin island-local

**Rationale**: Satori's rule model is island-local. Suijin must prove that local island state matters.

## Resolved Decisions

- Ku Seed places Void; placed Void separates islands.
- First alpha kami is Suijin.
- Suijin requires Chi+Ku biome placement on an island with at least 10 water tiles, no fire-based tiles, and local Satori 1000.
