# Research: First Island Fun Loop

## Decision: Prioritize clarity over content breadth

**Rationale**: The first island must feel alive before adding more spirits or structures. One polished helper structure is more valuable than many unclear forms.

## Decision: Keep duplicate ritual rejection central

**Rationale**: The ritual grammar says no duplicates should be allowed ever. The first-island loop is where players are most likely to test invalid combinations.

## Decision: Treat Satori as feedback, not only score

**Rationale**: The player needs to understand why housing and care matter. Satori should communicate pressure and recovery without requiring a spreadsheet.

## Resolved Decisions

- Dew Bowl and Wind Chime are both alpha helper structures.
- Satori feedback uses a combination: immediate HUD change, hover/inspect detail, and Codex meaning.
